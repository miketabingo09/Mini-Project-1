Your music, your community.

uPlay Music Center makes high quality music accessible to people of all ages, backgrounds and abilities. Classes are offered on a sliding scale.

uPlay students range in age from a few months to nearly 100 years old, and they enjoy classes in everything from Western classical to Chinese to Latin Music. Audience members enjoy events ranging from square dances to contemporary music performances. Last year more than 2,800 students of all ages studied at CMC and thousands enjoyed uPlay performances.

Our commitment to accessibility dates back a century

uPlay mission of accessibility drives every aspect of our work. Our tuition assistance program, nearly $2.8M last school year, supports students through free programs, scholarships, and sliding-scale tuition.

Founded in 1921, uPlay Music Center is one of the oldest and largest community arts organizations on the West Coast. From our teachers to our staff, we're all committed to sharing our love of music and helping our students succeed. uPlay has branches in the Mission and Richmond districts, as well as programs throughout the city.

uPlay programs at a glance

  • Group classes for adults in Music Theory, Composition, and Somatic Education
  • Online learning options available for distance learning
  • Instruction in diverse musical styles including Western classical, jazz, blues, gospel, Latin, and Chinese
  • Award-winning Young Musicians Programs: free private lesson and ensemble programs for diverse, primarily low-income youth
