GUITAR LESSON OVERVIEW

  • Basic chords and strumming patterns: This is usually the starting point for beginner guitar players. You will learn the basic chords and how to strum them in different patterns.
  • Fingerpicking: This technique involves plucking the strings with your fingers instead of using a pick. It's a great way to add texture and variety to your playing.
  • Scales and improvisation: Learning scales is essential for improvisation and creating melodies. You will learn different scales and how to use them to create solos and improvisations.
  • Music theory: Understanding music theory is essential for becoming a well-rounded musician. You will learn about rhythm, harmony, melody, and how they all work together.
  • Songwriting: Songwriting is a great way to express yourself creatively. You will learn how to write songs, create chord progressions, and structure your songs.
  • Advanced techniques: Once you have mastered the basics, you can move on to more advanced techniques such as sweep picking, tapping, and alternate picking.
  • Playing with others: Playing with other musicians is a great way to improve your skills and have fun. You will learn how to play with a band, how to jam, and how to improvise with others.

Apply Now

PIANO LESSON OVERVIEW

  • Music theory: Understanding music theory is essential for becoming a well-rounded musician. You will learn about rhythm, harmony, melody, and how they all work together.
  • Note reading and sight-reading: Learning to read sheet music is an essential skill for playing piano. You will learn how to read notes on the staff and how to play them on the piano.
  • Technique: Good technique is essential for playing piano well. You will learn how to position your hands, how to use proper finger placement, and how to play with the right amount of force.
  • Scales and arpeggios: Learning scales and arpeggios is essential for developing finger strength and dexterity. You will learn different scales and how to use them to create melodies and improvisations.
  • Chords and chord progressions: Understanding chords and chord progressions is essential for playing songs on the piano. You will learn how to play different chords and how to create chord progressions.
  • Playing with expression: Playing with expression is what makes a piece of music come alive. You will learn how to use dynamics, phrasing, and tempo to bring out the emotion in a piece of music.
  • Music history: Understanding the history of music is essential for becoming a well-rounded musician. You will learn about different periods of music, famous composers, and how music has evolved over time.
